OZORA FESTIVAL HISTORY :
OZORA
Festival
is
the
ultimate
psychedelic
Tribal
Gathering
and
undoubtedly
one
of
the
most
influential
psychedelic
trance
festivals
in
the
world.
Originating
as
a
celebration
of
the
1999
solar
eclipse,
the
festival
has
grown
to
become
one
of
the
most
immersive
and
interactive
experiences
in
the
world.
